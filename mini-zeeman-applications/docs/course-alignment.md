## Zeeman's Original Applications (covered in this module)

### Biology
- **Heartbeat** (Zeeman 1972): Cusp catastrophe governs diastole/systole transition
- **Nerve impulse** (Zeeman 1973): Action potential as cusp catastrophe
- **Cell differentiation** (Thom 1975, Zeeman 1974): Morphogen-driven fate decision

### Social Sciences
- **Prison riots** (Zeeman 1976): Tension + trigger → sudden riot
- **Stock market crashes** (Zeeman 1974): Speculation + mispricing → crash
- **War initiation** (Zeeman 1979): Threat + cost → sudden war

### Physics & Engineering
- **Euler buckling** (Zeeman 1976): Beam buckling as pitchfork/cusp
- **Phase transitions** (van der Waals): Cusp at critical point
- **Ship stability** (Zeeman 1977): Capsize as cusp catastrophe
